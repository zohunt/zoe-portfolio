About Me

Hey, I’m Zoe — a creative front-end web developer with a love for meaningful, aesthetic, and purpose-driven design. Whether I’m building a lunar journaling app like MoonMemo or crafting celestial layouts for streamers like LettersToLittleMe, I’m all about combining accessibility, elegance, and emotion in the browser.

I’m passionate about learning, especially in areas like full-stack development, and I’m working toward relocating to Germany 🌍. I love celestial motifs, gold accents, and making people feel *seen* when they land on a page I’ve built.

Right now, I’m sharpening my JavaScript skills, diving into backend fundamentals, and building a portfolio that reflects both my professional drive and personal creativity.

If you like expressive interfaces, thoughtful UX, or just want to talk about the moon 🌕 — let’s connect!
